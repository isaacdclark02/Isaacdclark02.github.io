Exploring Relationships between Lower Body STI and Force Plate Performance

Advanced statistical analysis was used as part of this project to develop a predictive model capable of predicting an individual athlete's risk of injury using countermovement jump metrics. Multiple methods and models were tested with various athlete's from different sports using various test metrics. Findings and conclusions were presented at the conclusion of the project and at the 2024 AHPS Symposium.

Investigating the Correlation Between Strength and Power Metrics in American Football

Our project objectives were to analyze existing data collected on collegiate football athletes to determine if any relationships between athlete's strength and power existed. Analysis and conclusions would be used to determine training efficasy between player groups. The creation of a simple SQL database was also part of this project. Findings and conclusions were presented at the conclusion of the project.
